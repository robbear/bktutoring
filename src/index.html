Linda Bearman Korth

A love for learning is a foundation for developing self-confidence, inner motivation, and future success. Reading success is key to building this foundation – as skills improve, so does a child’s confidence.

I have been an early childhood educator for more than 25 years, with an MA in Early Childhood Education/Special Education. My experience working with young children ranges from teacher to education specialist to program administrator training and supporting other teachers in a variety of learning situations.

Rob Bearman

Rob began his career teaching algebra full-time at San Mateo High School while working towards his Master's at Stanford, and then at Santa Cruz High School where he taught Algebra I, Algebra II, and Physics..

Rob earned his Master's degree in Education at Stanford in 1985 and has had a long career in software engineering, including a decade at Microsoft.
