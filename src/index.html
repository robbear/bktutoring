Rob Bearman

Whether in the high school classroom, or building software with teams at places like Microsoft, my favorite times in my career were helping others one-on-one, striving to see their perspective as a way of bringing new understandings. Teaching is a way to broaden my own learning, and I enjoy the deeper insights about mathematics that come to me through the eyes of those I help.

I earned a Master's degree in Education at Stanford after studying Astronomy and Physics at UC Berkeley. I taught first and second year Algebra along with Physics in the 1980s before joining the software industry. I am fascinated with the history and practice of mathematics, and I want to help students by giving them a broader perspective of their mathematics learning through their own interests and self-awareness.
